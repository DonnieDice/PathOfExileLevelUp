# RGX | Path of Exile Level-Up! ## ![](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/media/kiwi.gif?raw=true) RGX Mods - RealmGX Community Project *** ## Overview **RGX | Path of Exile Level-Up!** plays a Path of Exile-inspired level-up chime whenever your character levels up in World of Warcraft. ![RealmGX Kiwi](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/media/kiwi.gif?raw=true) **The Kiwi Says:** "Exile ding! Bwwiiiee." *** ## Deprecation Notice This standalone addon is preserved for existing users. Its sound has also been merged into [BLU | Better Level Up!](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/blu-better-level-up) and [BLU Classic | Better Level Up!](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/blu-classic). *** ## Features Automatic playback - The custom sound plays when you gain a level. Default ding muting - WoW's default level-up sound is muted on login. Lightweight - Single-sound addon with no embedded libraries. Multi-Version - Includes support files for Retail, Wrath, Burning Crusade, and Classic Era. *** ## Command Reference No slash commands are required. Enable the addon and it works automatically. *** ## Installation 1. **Download** RGX | POELU from your preferred platform: * [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/pathofexilelevelup) * [Wago.io](https://addons.wago.io/addons/pathofexilelevelup) * [GitHub](https://github.com/DonnieDice/PathOfExileLevelUp) 2. **Extract** the addon folder to your WoW `Interface/AddOns` directory. 3. **Restart** WoW and enable the addon. ![RealmGX Kiwi](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/media/kiwi.gif?raw=true) **The Kiwi Says:** "Installation complete.
